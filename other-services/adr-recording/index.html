ADR & Dialogue Recording

Precision Dialogue for Film, TV, and Streaming Productions.

At MNK Studios, we offer the only dedicated environment for ADR and dialogue recording in the UAE, built to meet the technical and creative standards of modern film and television production. Whether for feature films, streaming content, or advertising, our setup ensures pristine vocal capture perfectly matched to picture.

Our ADR workflow is fully integrated with Dante-enabled routing and Avid Pro Tools Ultimate, allowing seamless synchronization to picture playback and precise cue management. Each session is conducted in a Dolby-designed and calibrated Atmos control room, providing accurate translation of spatial detail and tonal balance.

We provide professional-grade microphones, transparent signal paths, and an acoustically optimized recording booth for clean, consistent sound. Our engineers work closely with directors and talent, whether they’re in-studio or connected remotely, to achieve authentic performances that align perfectly with the scene.

Remote ADR sessions are supported through high-quality, low-latency links, enabling international productions to record talent from anywhere in the world while monitoring in real time at MNK Studios.
