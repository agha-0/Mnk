The Lightroom

A versatile space for film, photography, and performance.

A versatile creative space for film, photography, and performance.

The Lightroom is MNK Studios’ most adaptable creative space; a bright, open 1500 sqft. area designed to accommodate a wide range of visual and performance productions. From music videos and photo shoots to creative showcases and small-scale events, The Lightroom offers a blank canvas where ideas can come to life.

Flooded with natural light and built with the same attention to sound treatment as the rest of MNK Studios, this space delivers both beauty and functionality. It’s perfectly suited for productions that demand flexibility whether that means building a set, hosting a brand shoot, or recording a live performance with full PA and lighting rigs.

Ideal For

  • Music video and performance shoots
  • Photo and commercial productions
  • Brand or content campaigns
  • Rehearsals, showcases, and small events
  • Creative workshops and exhibitions

Key Features

  • Spacious open-plan layout with natural skylight
  • Acoustically treated environment
  • Supports large lighting and PA setups
  • Suitable for photography, film, and live content production
  • Flexible rental options (hourly, daily, or project-based)

Whether you’re capturing an artist performance, producing a visual campaign, or staging a creative event, The Lightroom offers the space, technical freedom, and professional support to bring your vision to life.

The Loft

A refined space for relaxation, dining, and productivity.

A refined, multi-purpose space crafted for effortless relaxation, comfortable dining, and focused productivity.

The Loft is right next to the Lightroom, and primarily serves as a space for relaxation for artists who’ve had a few hours of work to do in the sound studios. Equipped with a pantry to make yourself a cup of tea or coffee, or order food in to dine or just relax and watch TV or play some games altogether, the Loft is a space that simulates a living room; a place to chill.

Alternatively, the Loft is also a space that can be exclusively yours to use for other work besides music. Whether you’re a tour manager with a team managing a touring band, or a production manager with a team at the facility for a video shoot, this space more than serves your purpose with the amenities that come with it.

Ideal For

  • Music video and performance shoots
  • Photo and commercial productions
  • Brand or content campaigns
  • Workspace usage

Key Features

  • Spacious layout with natural skylight
  • Self-service pantry
  • Multiple seating areas and power outlets
  • Flexible rental options (hourly, daily, or project-based)
