Studio 1

Precision meets creativity.

Studio 1 is our flagship control and mixing suite designed for professional audio production, mixing, mastering, and sound design. Built with precision acoustics and complete isolation, the studio offers an environment of absolute sonic accuracy; ideal for both stereo and immersive workflows.

Connected via a fully integrated Dante network to the facility’s control rooms and live room, Studio 1 can capture, route, and process complex multitrack sessions in real time. Whether producing a record, mixing for film and OTT, or refining a master, the room’s infrastructure ensures seamless signal flow and uncompromising quality.

Every element from its monitoring and acoustic design to its digital routing and hardware chain has been engineered to meet the highest professional standards. The result is a creative space that translates perfectly between inspiration and execution, empowering artists, producers, and engineers to achieve their most refined sound.

Treatment: Fully decoupled “room-within-a-room” construction, offering exceptional isolation (~-55 dB attenuation).

Calibration: Calibrated for both stereo and immersive mixing environments with controlled decay times and balanced low-frequency response.

Monitoring Position: Centralized reference axis for flat response and phase coherence for stereo and immersive workflows.
