Studio B

The Flagship Rehearsal and Production Space at MNK Studios.

Studio B is the largest and most versatile space at our facility. It is a 650 square-foot acoustically treated room designed to accommodate everything from full band rehearsals to live recording sessions and video productions. Built to deliver professional sound and maximum comfort, it combines cutting-edge technology with an inspiring, creative atmosphere.

The room comfortably hosts up to 20 musicians, making it ideal for large ensembles, bands, or even chamber orchestras. Every element of Studio B has been designed for efficiency; from its pre-wired setup and sound isolation to its Dante-enabled network that connects directly with adjacent control rooms for live recording or hybrid production workflows.

Studio B features a full professional backline, including a Pearl Masters Maple Complete drum kit, Blackstar, Ampeg, and Orange amplifiers, along with a high-performance Allen & Heath Avantis 64-channel digital console for seamless audio control. The room also includes a powerful PA and monitoring system, adjustable LED lighting to set the mood, and flexible recording options, from stereo room capture to full multitrack recording through the studio’s integrated Dante system and our Neve 1073OPX interface for the most detailed capture of drum recordings.

Whether you’re rehearsing for tour, recording a live performance, or shooting a music video, Studio B provides the space, sound, and technology to make it happen with ease.
