Studio S is one of two similar-sized rehearsal rooms that offers a dynamic, fully equipped space designed for musicians and bands who want more than just practice. Studio S allows you to capture your rehearsals directly in stereo or multitrack formats, with seamless routing to the main control rooms. It’s not just a practice space; it’s a creative environment engineered for artists who want to play,experiment, and evolve their sound in a professional-grade setting.
