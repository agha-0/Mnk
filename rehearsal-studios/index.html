At MNK Studios, we’ve redefined what a rehearsal space can be.

Our three purpose-built live studios in Dubai are fully soundproofed and acoustically treated, designed to deliver the perfect environment for musicians to create, rehearse, and record with ease.

Each room is pre-miked and ready to play, featuring professional-grade drums, amplifiers, and microphones. All you need to bring are your guitars, pedals, and cymbals. The setup is seamless with the aid of our team of professional audio engineers, letting you focus entirely on your sound.

We include live stereo recording with every session free of charge and offer multitrack recording as an add-on for artists who want to capture every detail of their performance.

Need extra gear? We also rent out gear upon request. Just reach out for availability and rates.
