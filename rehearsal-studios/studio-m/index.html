Studio M

Rehearse with power. Create with confidence.

Studio M is a premium rehearsal space at MNK Studios, designed to accommodate full-band setups with professional-level gear, excellent acoustic isolation, and built-in recording options. At approximately 340 sq ft, it comfortably supports 6 to 7-piece bands, giving you enough space to rehearse, experiment and refine your performance in a dedicated environment.

The room is fully pre-wired: drums, amps, microphones and PA/monitor system are already in place so you only need to bring your guitars, pedals and cymbals. With live stereo recording included by default (and optional multitrack recording available), it bridges rehearsal and capturing your sound-in-the-moment.

Studio M features a 16-channel digital mixer and a powerful PA/monitor system, all set up for quick turnaround and minimal setup time. The vibe is creative, focused and professional, which is ideal for bands who take their rehearsals seriously and want more value.
